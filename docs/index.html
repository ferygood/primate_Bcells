Intro

We use TEKRABber to analyze the differntially expressed orthologs and also analyze the correlations of KRAB-ZNFs and TEs. The data comes from human, chimpanzee, and orangutans B-cells RNA-seq data (3 biological replicates/ each species). We comparing three species using human as references.
